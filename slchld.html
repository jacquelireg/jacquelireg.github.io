Simulated Environment

May 2018 | Soundscape | Adobe Premiere Pro

An experiement with an assortment of audio files found online and different audio effects in Premiere to layer sounds and create a fake environment that sounded real. The fake world should be comparable to someone outside in the city on a rainy night, playing their music aloud on their phone with the sounds of cars and disant sirens in the background. The tone set by the sounds should make the listener feel calm, yet melancholic, as though they're missing something from their life.

Jack-o-Lantern

October 2017 | 3D Printed Sculpture | Minecraft, Mineways, Meshmixer, Cura, 3D printer

A 3D printed jack-o-lantern that I first built in Minecraft and then transferred into an assortment of programs to get it printed. I chose this object in particular because of its cultural significance and how it is usually considered the main symbol of Halloween.

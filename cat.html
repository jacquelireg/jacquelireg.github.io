Melting Cat

November 2018 | Sculpture | Steel Sheet Metal + Welding

A scultpure of a melting cat with only it's head, front arms and tail sticking out of the puddle. It was meant to be a physical representation of my cat allergies and my inability to own one because of this. Thus, my dreams of getting a cat are melting away.

Memento Mori

March 2020 | 3D Modeling | Autodesk Maya

For this work, I considered the scenario of a virtual gallery that encourages thought about what happens to your personal identity in the world after you die. Will your name be remembered for years to come or will you be another erased person in the list of people who have existed on this Earth? Everything in space will die without protection, whether it's through a time lapse of degradation or through a barrage of asteroids. And by being "trapped" in this simulated space knowing that one way or another you're coming to an end, it gives you time to reflect on your life. I kept with the theme of the physical body by using organs, skeletons, wallpapers of CT and MRI scans so that it fits with the idea of the destruction of the physical self.

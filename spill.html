Spill

March 29 2019 | Digital Video | Adobe Premiere Pro

A short mockup for a video mapping workshop. It was intended to be projected onto a pool or some body of water, and is meant to be a representative of ocean pollution and oil spills by blending together videos of aquatic life and colored abstractions.

Simulated Environment

September 2018 | Soundscape | Adobe Premiere Pro

I wanted to experiement with an assortment of audio files found online and different audio effects in Premiere to layer sounds and create a fake environment that sounded real. The fake world I was going for was someone outside in the city on a rainy night, playing their music aloud on their phone with the sounds of cars and disant sirens in the background. I was trying to go for a calm yet melancholic mood.

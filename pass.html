This Too Shall Pass

April 2019 | Digital Video | Adobe Premiere Pro

This is an experimental documentary on how two people view life as college students. For this project I chose not to work with a script or storyboard aside from the interview questions I asked because I wanted to retain the candidness of the interviewees and the scenery they grew up with. Asking them questions they didn't know beforehand removed the filter they would've normally had when answering. All of the editing and camera work in the video was done by myself

Interviewing a Street Fighter Fanatic

May 2018 | Video Art | Adobe Premiere Pro

A video in which I interview a friend to get to the bottom of why he loves playing a game that's full of toxic people. Why continue to spend time and energy on a game that can be emotionally/physically draining and is full of trash-talking elitist players? And maybe the answers will give insights to other players' mindsets in different games.

The Weight of Words

April 2018 | Net Art | StepWorks

A small text adventure that showcases the impact certain phrases can have and stick with me for many years. A lot of words are heard and spoken every day, but there are only a handful of phrases that I have kept in my memories and have affected different aspects of my life, as well as the way I reflect on myself.

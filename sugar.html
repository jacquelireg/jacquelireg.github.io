Sugar

May 2018 | Video Art | Adobe Premiere Pro

An exploration into the mindset of a person in a toxic relationship. The prevalent use of sugar is meant to represent the dynamic of this type of relationship, from the over indulgence in the substance to being physically hurt by something that is generally considered sweet.

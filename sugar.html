Sugar

May 2018 | Digital Video | Adobe Premiere Pro

I made this video as an exploration into the mindset of a person in a toxic relationship. The prevalent use of sugar is meant to represent the dynamic of this type of relationship, from the over indulgence in the substance to being physically hurt by something that is generally considered sweet. The camera work and editing was done by me for this project.

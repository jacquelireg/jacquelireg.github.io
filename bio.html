Bio

Jacqueline Regalado is a digital media artist based in the Northern Bay Area. Her work is focused around video art and exploring the feeling of wanting to belong somewhere or figuring out how to visualize emotions that aren't commonly seen. She is a student at San Jose State University in her final year and will graduate in May 2020 with a BFA in Digital Media. Her work can be seen regularly in group exhibitions at various SJSU galleries. She has worked Seattle Web Search and Helpsy Health for a total of ten months as a video editor intern.

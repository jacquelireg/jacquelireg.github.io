Color Palette

March 2018 | Interactive Net Art | JavaScript

An interactive piece based around the idea that the most memorable characters also have the most memorable color palettes, so I wanted to see if the characters from Doki Doki Literature Club were easily recognized just based on the colors they were giving. I also wanted it to double as a form of relaxation; watching the colors mix and go over each other can be very soothing. Click here to try it out yourself.

Here's to the Little Enjoyments

September 2018 | Digital Video | Adobe Premiere Pro

A reminder to myself not to get so caught up in being stressed out, and to just focus on the good things, no matter how little they may seem. Some of my favorite "little things" are presented here as spending time with loved ones, collecting graphic socks, and riding my board. I cropped the video to represent the idea of myself focusing on something and the grain/noise as me reflecting on how I enjoyed my time.

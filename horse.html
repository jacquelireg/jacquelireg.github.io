Lasercut Horse

November 2018 | Sculpture | 1/8" Wood + Illustrator + Lasercutter

A small rocking horse I made for an assignment for a 3D Design class. I sketched out the image I had in mind for the horse and then did a vector drawing in Illustrator, using the color red to mean cuts and blue for raster etching on the lasercutter.
